Consumption vs Creation

Content outline. Why write the essay, what's included, what point am I making.

The Endless Provider

The internet as an addictive, endless provider of content that is specifically engineered to attract and maintain my attention.

Honing the Outlets

Why is creating content valuable? Creative outlet, sharing ideas, exploring and organizing my mind.

Outlets that I have chosen to focus on, including music and photography.

Finding the Balance

The internet is both the driving factor for my overconsumption and fuel for my creation. A platform for sharing.

Do not eliminate the consumption of content, but instead focus it on the right things that benefit me the most.
